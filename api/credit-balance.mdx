---
title: "Legacy Wallet Credit Balance"
api: "GET /credits/{id}/balance"
---

Get the legacy Lago wallet credit balance for a customer.

**Auth:** customer session with `credits:read`, or a secret key. Publishable keys are always rejected.

<Warning>
This is not a product Credit System balance. New integrations should use [Customer Credit Balances](/api/customer-credit-balances).
</Warning>

<ParamField path="id" type="string" required>
  External customer ID
</ParamField>

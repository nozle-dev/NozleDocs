---
title: "Cancel Subscription"
api: "DELETE /subscriptions/{id}"
---

Cancel a subscription. The cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period -- the customer retains access until then.

**Auth:** `pk_` (publishable key) or `sk_` (secret key).

<ParamField path="id" type="string" required>
  The subscription ID to cancel.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="reason" type="string">
  Optional cancellation reason for your records.
</ParamField>

### Response

Returns the updated subscription with a `canceled` status and the `ends_at` timestamp indicating when access expires.
